How this works:

Select the time that you want by clicking on the link below the time slot. Each box represents a table, which can hold up to 4 people. You cannot select a specific table.
Specifics:

- if your party is 3-4 people, your table will be "closed" unless there are people on the wait list.
- if your party is less than 3 people, you will be labelled a "community table" which others will be placed at until the table is full
- if your party is larger than the amount of slots alloted during the time selected, you will be redirected and asked to select another time
